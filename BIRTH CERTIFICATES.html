Maharashtra Right to Public Services

Government of Maharashtra

Service Name: Senior Citizen Certificate (Service ID: 2476)

Required Documents (Mandatory)

  • Identity Proof (Any One): PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport, or RSBY Card.
  • Address Proof (Any One): Passport, Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Ration Card, Water Bill, or Electricity Bill.
  • Age Proof (Any One): Birth Certificate, School Leaving Certificate, Bonafide Certificate, or Civil Surgeon Medical Certificate.

Additional Documents

  • Passport size photograph of the applicant.
  • Self-declaration form (duly signed).
  • Proof of being a resident of Maharashtra (Domicile or long-term stay proof).
Important Note: Please ensure all documents are scanned in high resolution (PDF/JPEG) as per the size limits specified in the application portal. No physical visit to the office is required unless mandated by the designated officer.
