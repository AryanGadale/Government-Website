Revenue Department

The Revenue Department is the backbone of the district administration. It acts as a representative of the government for the common man. The department is headed by the District Collector.

  • Land Administration: Maintenance of land records (7/12, 8A), mutation entries, and land measurement.
  • Collection of Dues: Collection of Non-Agricultural (NA) tax, royalty on minor minerals, and other government dues.
  • Law and Order: Executive Magistracy duties to maintain peace and order in the district.
  • Disaster Management: Relief and rehabilitation operations during floods, droughts, and other natural calamities.
  • Elections: Conducting Lok Sabha, Vidhan Sabha, and local body elections.
