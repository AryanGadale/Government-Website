Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

The PMFBY is a flagship scheme to provide insurance coverage and financial support to farmers in the event of failure of any of the notified crops as a result of natural calamities, pests, and diseases. [Official Portal üîó]

Objectives Read Guidelines üîó

üéØ
  • To provide financial support to farmers suffering crop loss/damage arising out of unforeseen events.
  • To stabilize the income of farmers to ensure their continuance in farming.
  • To encourage farmers to adopt innovative and modern agricultural practices.

Farmers can apply for crop insurance through the following channels:

  1. Nearest Bank Branch / PACs (Primary Agricultural Credit Society).
  2. Common Service Centers (CSC) / Aaple Sarkar Kendra.
  3. Directly on the PMFBY Portal or Crop Insurance App.
