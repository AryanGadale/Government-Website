About District Pune

Pune district is one of the most important districts in the state of Maharashtra. It is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, educational institutions, IT industry, and rapid urban development. Pune has been a center of social, political, and educational movements since ancient times.

The district plays a vital role in administration, industry, agriculture, and tourism, contributing significantly to the overall development of the state.
